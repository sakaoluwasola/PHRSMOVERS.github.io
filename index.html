ABOUT US

Making Your Move Quick and Easy

PHRS MOVERS is a moving/removalist company, a professional service provider that specializes in helping individuals and businesses relocate their belongings from one place to another. We offer a range of services to facilitate a smooth and efficient moving process for our clients. We typically offer a variety of services to cater to different customer needs. These services include packing and unpacking, loading and unloading, transportation of goods, disassembly and reassembly of furniture, storage facilities, and specialized services for delicate or valuable items.

PHRS MOVERS is one of the prominent moving company in Nigeria operating since 2020. We are licensed Movers in the country and have been rendering these services for years all over the nation. All our staff are experts in moving with skilled labor as we make moving easy and hassle-free.
