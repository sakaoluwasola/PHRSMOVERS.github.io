Residential and Commercial Moves

We handle both residential and commercial moves. We assist individuals and families who are relocating their homes, as well as businesses and offices that are shifting to new locations.

Local and Long-Distance Moves

We handle both local and long-distance moves. Local moves typically involve relocating within the same city or a short distance away, while long-distance moves involve crossing state lines or even international borders. For long-distance moves. PHRS MOVERS may use various modes of transportation, such as trucks, trains, ships, or airplanes for adequate moving of our client belongings.

Packing and Unpacking

We offer packing and unpacking services to save our clients time and effort. We bring packing materials, such as boxes, bubble wrap, and packing tape, and professionally pack the belongings, ensuring their safety during transportation. Upon arrival at the new location, we can also assist with unpacking and setting up the items.

Insurance and Liability

As a reputable moving company in Nigeria we provide insurance coverage for the items being transported. This coverage protects against any damage or loss that may occur during the moving process. It's important to clarify the extent of coverage and liability before hiring services.

Storage Facilities

We have our own storage facilities. We also partner with storage providers. These facilities offer short-term or long-term storage solutions for clients who require temporary storage of their belongings before, during, or after a move.

Specialized Services

As a moving company we offer specialized services for specific items that require extra care. For example, we have expertise in moving pianos, fine art, antiques, or fragile electronic equipment. These specialized services often involve additional cost provision and equipment to ensure the safe handling of such items.

Moving Out Painting

We will help you move out of your old apartment and still give it a new look. We offer you the best painting service available by our skilled and adequately structured professionals.

OUR PROCESS

Our streamlined
process ensures a
smooth transition

1

Assessment and Quotation

We conduct a thorough assessment of the customer's belongings to determine the scope of the move and provide an accurate quotation. This step helps set expectations for both parties and ensures transparency in pricing.

2

Packing and Secure Handling

We carefully pack and secure the customer's items using proper materials and techniques to prevent damage during transportation. Label boxes appropriately to ensure easy identification and organized unpacking.

3

Unloading and Placement

Once at the destination, unload the items with care and place them in the designated areas or rooms according to the customer's instructions. This step helps facilitate a smooth transition for the customer and minimizes the effort required for unpacking.

4

Transportation and Logistics

Efficiently coordinate the transportation of goods from the origin to the destination. This involves selecting the appropriate vehicles, planning routes, and managing logistics to ensure timely and safe delivery.
